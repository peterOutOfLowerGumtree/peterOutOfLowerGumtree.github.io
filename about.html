About Me

This is who I am.

Profile

I have always been very numerate and good with logic puzzles, and in school my friends and I discovered very basic programming in Xcode on the library computers, where we tried to make an RSS app. It was this and an interest in circuitry and electronics which led me to choose a degree in electrical & electronic engineering, where I would be able to satisfy my desire to learn how to successfully code an app while still being able to do something tactile.

Throughout my degree, I excelled in the coding modules, and for my final year project I combined my interest in coding with my love for music to make a digital multi-effects guitar pedal with an Arduino Due as the microcontroller. While I was researching the literature review for my FYP, I stumbled across OOP and a tutorial on designing a chess game entirely from scratch in Java. This excited me and research into the world of Java and IT led me to decide that I wanted to pursue a career in IT where I could use my logical reasoning skills and experience in coding, and really delve further into the world of programming.

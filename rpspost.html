Rock Paper Scissors Project

For the Rock Paper Scissors group project, we needed to simulate a game of Rock Paper Scissors vs a computer. We also had to display various statistics about the game, have a maximum number of turns, be able to reset the game, and add extra nerdy-af options to the game (lizard and Spock).

We didn't get a chance to add an AI for the computer, and at this stage the computer just picks an option randomly. To implement an AI, the computer would have to guess the user's most probable next move based on previous inputs in order to attempt to counter the user (eg: if the user plays Rock three times then the computer could play Paper due to counter the player's pattern of picking Rock).
